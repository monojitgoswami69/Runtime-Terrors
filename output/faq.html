info General Questions

AmbuQuick is India's first AI-powered ambulance aggregator platform. We connect you with the nearest available ambulance in under 8 minutes using intelligent dispatch technology, real-time hospital bed tracking, and automated family notifications.

While 108 is a government service, AmbuQuick offers:

  • Faster response: 8 minutes avg vs 20-30 minutes
  • Real-time tracking: Know exactly where your ambulance is
  • Hospital bed availability: We route to hospitals with available beds
  • Transparent pricing: Know costs upfront, no hidden charges
  • Family notifications: Automatic SMS/WhatsApp alerts to emergency contacts
  • AI triage: Right ambulance type (BLS/ALS/ICU) for your emergency

Currently available in: Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune, Kolkata, and Ahmedabad. We're rapidly expanding to 20+ cities by 2026.
